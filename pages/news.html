Hayden Announces New Album Release Date

December 3, 2024

Fans rejoice! Hayden Hester, has officially announced the release date for his highly anticipated album, City Lights and Daydreams. The album, set to drop this spring, April 24th, 2025, promises a mix of soulful ballads and upbeat anthems that capture Hayden's signature sound. "This album is my heart in music form," Hayden shared. "I can't wait for the world to hear it".
